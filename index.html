Sri Basaveshwara Gayatri Temple

I had visited it during May 2022.

Located in Bangaluru is the beautiful Gayatri temple with idols of Lords Ganesha, Shiva, Hanuman and Goddess Lakshmi, with amazing architecture.



Gateway of India

I visted this place in 2019.

The Gateway of India is a popular tourist attraction in Mumbai as it was built to commemorate the visit of King George V and Queen Mary to India.



hawa mahal

I visited this place in December 2021

The Hawa Mahal is a palace in the city of Jaipur, built from red and pink sandstone.The structure was built in 1799 by the Maharaja Sawai Pratap Singh, grandson of Maharaja Sawai Jai Singh, the founder of the city of Jaipur, India.

Movie Reviews

Murder On Orient Express
Amazing movie to watch for mystery and suspense lovers.

The elegant train of the 1930s, the Orient Express, is stopped by heavy snowfall. A murder is discovered, and Poirot's trip home to London from the Middle East is interrupted to solve the case. The opening chapters of the novel take place in Istanbul. The rest of the novel takes place in Yugoslavia, with the train trapped between Vinkovci and Brod. It is a mysterious movie which creates suspense among the characters.

Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Askaban
My favourite one out of the 8 Harry Potter movies.

Harry, Ron and Hermoine return to Hogwarts just as they learn about Sirius Black and his plans to kill Harry. However, when Harry runs into him, he learns that the truth is far from reality. It tells us more about Harry's past and reavals what really happened.
